Loop-La-Loop

A practical project that helps refugees become part of local communities

Youmanity reports on an inspiring human story. A group of refugees have learned to crochet to make comforting teddy bears for refugee children. Actually, these entrepreneurial crocheters are making teddies for ALL children who want to adopt one. Each teddy comes with his own name and carry a beautiful 

message of encouragement for the children in their back pack. The message reads: “Be curious and eager to learn. Never give up. Because Education is Freedom!" To adopt a bear is simple. All you need is love and an address where you would like the bear to travel to. For more information on how to adopt a bear.
